--CUTTING LIVE TO THIS "BLACK MASK," APPARENTLY RESPONSIBLE FOR THE HORRIFIC SPIKE IN--
HELLO, GOTHAM. MY NAME IS ROMAN.
A LITTLE ABOUT ME. I GREW UP ON A PIG FARM. HUMBLE BEGINNINGS, I KNOW. ONE OF MY JOBS ON OUR FARM WAS TO KILL THE PIGS WHEN THE TIME CAME.
I USED ONE OF THOSE CAPTIVE BOLT PISTOLS. FIVE-INCH PIN RIGHT IN THE HEAD. MOST TIMES IT WAS JUST "THUNK." DOWN.
BUT ONCE IN A WHILE, ON RARE OCCASIONS, THE PIG WOULD HAVE THIS... SPASM? NOT OF PAIN, BUT OF JOY. IT WAS WILD. IT'D LET OUT THIS MOAN AND SHIVER AND I'D EVEN SMILE, WITH BLOOD SPURTING FROM ITS HEAD.
MAR...
WHAT IS IT?
I LEARNED LATER THAT THIS ONLY HAPPENED IF THE PIN GOT DEEP ENOUGH TO HIT THIS TINY LITTLE SPOT IN THE BRAIN CALLED THE HEDONISTIC HUB.
THE MAIN PLEASURE CENTER.
AND THAT ONLY OCCURRED IF THE PIG DIDN'T TENSE UP WHEN THE PIN HIT, BUT JUST RELAXED AND ACCEPTED IT.
IF THE PIG DID, THE HUB WOULD LITERALLY FLOOD THE LAST BITS OF ITS BRAIN WITH TOTAL, UNCHECKED PLEASURE. THIS SPLIT SECOND OF ECSTASY THAT ONLY CAME WITH THE ACCEPTANCE OF DEATH.
WHICH BRINGS ME TO NOW, THIS MOMENT. BECAUSE... COME ON. WE ALL FEEL IT. THE BIG GUYS, THEY KILLED THE WORLD LONG AGO.
...AND YET THEY KEEP US ALL SLAVING AWAY WITH THE PROMISE IT'S ALL STILL ALIVE. I MEAN, DAMMIT, YOU CAN BARELY PAY YOUR BILLS, WHILE THEIR MONEY MAKES MONEY. YOU DRAG RECYCLING TO THE CURB WHILE THEY RIDE IN PRIVATE JETS THAT TEAR THE SKY APART.
YOU STRUGGLE TO HOLD THIS SYSTEM TOGETHER THAT DOES NOTHING FOR YOU, DOING THE RIGHT THING SO THEY CAN DO THE WRONG ONE?
WHY?
