ONCE UPON A TIME THAT WAS AND WAS NOT...
...WHEN THE STARS WHISPERED OF FUTURE-FORGOTTEN HUES...
...IN A LARGE GOLDEN CASTLE LIVED A PRINCESS AND HER GLOOMY SHADOW.
NO MATTER HOW HARD THE PRINCESS TRIED, SHE COULD NOT STOP HER SHADOW FROM CRYING.
ZATANNA
#1 PREVIEW
WRITER AND ARTIST JAMAL CAMPBELL
LETTERER ARIANA MAHER
SUPERMAN CREATED BY JERRY SIEGEL AND JOE SHUSTER, BY SPECIAL ARRANGEMENT WITH THE JERRY SIEGEL FAMILY.
DESPERATE FOR AID, THE PRINCESS APPROACHED THE VILLAGE OF MAGI LIVING BELOW HER CASTLE.
THE MAGI CAST A MIGHTY SPELL UPON THE PRINCESS, AND HER SHADOW BEGAN TO RISE.
THUS, ONE PRINCESS BECAME TWO; A SISTER OF LIGHT AND A SISTER OF SHADOW.
